Welcome to Finance@Condair

Condair is the world’s leading manufacturer of commercial and industrial humidification and evaporative cooling products and systems, setting standards with regard to energy efficiency and hygienic solutions. We export to over 50 countries throughout the world. Our important own product brands are: Condair, Nortec, Defensor, Draabe and ML System. Condair is represented in India's textile, tobacco and abattoir industries by Regent Machine and Servicing, and in the HVAC and pharmaceutical industries by Humidity Technologies Pvt Ltd. Both companies offer system design, supply, installation and servicing nationwide.
